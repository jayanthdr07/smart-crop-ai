Fertilizers & Their Uses

Urea

Use: Increases nitrogen in soil for leafy growth

Prevents/Treats: Yellowing of leaves due to nitrogen deficiency

BUY

Potash (KCl)

Use: Improves fruit quality and overall crop health

Prevents/Treats: Weak stems, poor fruiting

BUY

Phosphorus (Single Super Phosphate)

Use: Promotes root development and flowering

Prevents/Treats: Poor root growth, delayed flowering

BUY

Neem Cake

Use: Organic fertilizer that also controls pests

Prevents/Treats: Root-knot nematodes, fungal infections

BUY

Compost/Organic Manure

Use: Improves soil structure and microbial activity

Prevents/Treats: Soil nutrient deficiency, weak crop growth

BUY
